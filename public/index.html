Object Detector

How to use the App

  1. Wait for "Choose file" button to appear.
  2. Then press "Choose File" button to select an image from your device.
  3. After selecting the image, click on the "Detect Objects" button.
  4. For each object detected, there will appear a highlighted box.
  5. You can use options below to adjust final results.
